About

In today's world, the leading cause of death among developed countries is cardiovascular disease. Cardiovascular diseases caused by atherosclerotic lesions of the main arteries (coronary arteries, brain) are the main cause of death worldwide: no other cause of death causes as many people die annually as from heart disease.

Early prediction of illness is therefore a prerequisite for a happy and long life. And to solve this problem, research has been carried out and a website has been developed to forecast heart disease.
